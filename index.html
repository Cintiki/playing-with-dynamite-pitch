My read on the brief

Corporate safe. Cinematic special. Weird enough to stick.

This should not feel like a normal corporate keynote deck. It should feel like a clean executive system that got handed a film slate, a county fair ribbon, and a box of old school photos.

The corporate structure makes the room trust it. The cinematic language makes it feel important. The nostalgic weirdness makes it memorable.

Visual system

A pitch deck language with stage polish and county fair soul.

The system should feel structured enough for executives and tactile enough to carry Sean’s offbeat story world.

Color

Bold poster headlines, clean keynote body copy, typewriter labels, handwritten accents.

Tape, paper scraps, clapperboards, ribbon badges, note cards, old photos, film-strip edges.
